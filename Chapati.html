Chapati Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • Water, as needed for kneading
  • Salt, to taste
  • Ghee or oil, for cooking

Instructions

How to Make chapati at Home

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine 2 cups of whole wheat flour with a pinch of salt.
  2. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for about 15-20 minutes.
  3. Heat a tawa or skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook for about 30 seconds to 1 minute on one side until bubbles start to form.
  5. Continue cooking until both sides have golden brown spots, flipping as needed.
