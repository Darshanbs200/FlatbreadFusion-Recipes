Ragi Dosa

Ingredients

  • ½ cup of ragi flour
  • ¼ cup of rice flour
  • ¼ cup of Rava (also known as sooji)
  • 3 ½ cups of water
  • 2 green chilies finely chopped up
  • ½ teaspoon of crushed Whole Black Peppercorns
  • ½ teaspoon of crushed Whole Black Peppercorns
  • Salt to taste
  • Ghee

Instructions

How to Make Ragi Dosa at Home

  1. Mix sooji, rice flour, ragi, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add 3 cups of water and stir until thick.
  3. Let it rest for 10 minutes.
  4. Add remaining ingredients, mix well.
  5. Add ½ cup water for thinner consistency.
  6. Heat skillet, pour batter.
  7. Serve with yogurt or chutney.
