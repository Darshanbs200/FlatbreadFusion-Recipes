Akki Roti Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Rice Flour Fine Variety
  • 1 tsp Salt Adjust to taste
  • 1/4 Cup Dill Leaves Finely chopped
  • 1/4 Cup Carrot Grated
  • 1 Medium Onion Finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp Coriander Finely chopped
  • Few Curry Leaves Finely chopped
  • 2 to 3 Green Chillies Finely chopped
  • 1 inch Ginger Grated
  • 1/4 Cup Fresh Coconut Shredded
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • Water as required
  • Oil as needed for cooking Roti

Instructions

LET'S MAKE THE DOUGH:

  1. To a large bowl, add 2 cups rice flour.
  2. Mix thoroughly until the onions release moisture.
  3. Gradually add about Â¾ cup of water while mixing until a soft, pliable dough forms.
  4. Cover the dough and let it rest for at least 20 minutes.
  5. Cover and cook until the eggs are done to your liking.
  6. Cook until golden on both sides.
